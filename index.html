Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How does this work?

StudyPal is a platform that matches students with other students who are taking the same classes. This allows students to better study together.

First, a student would complete this survey applying themselves to be involved in a group (usually four), with questions that help improve group dynamics.

After Completing the Survey

When the student submits their application, they will receive a confirmation text and start matching with other students who are taking the same classes with an algorithm that finds a near-perfect match. The student will be able to see the other students' information and contact them to study together.

What if I don't See My Match Immediately?

Don't worry! Sometimes, it may take a little longer to find your perfect match. If all specified teams are currently at full capacity, or there aren't any viable options, we will notify you ASAP.
